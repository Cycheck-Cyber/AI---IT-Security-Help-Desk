Why This Matters
I
Shadow AI Is Already Here
Your employees are pasting source code, customer records, contracts, and sensitive documents into AI tools faster than procurement can approve them. Most organisations cannot name a single AI tool in daily use that has been properly reviewed.
II
Your DPO Cannot Answer the Audit
Cross-border transfer rules demand documented safeguards. But if your compliance team cannot name the tool, cannot tell where the data is processed, and cannot produce the paperwork — they cannot satisfy an audit, a customer question, or a data-subject request.
III
"EU-Branded" Doesn't Mean EU-Processed
An AI assistant with a European website may silently forward every prompt to a US-operated model. The front door wears an EU flag; the actual processing crosses the Atlantic. Without visibility into what sits behind the branding, your report measures the wrong thing.
