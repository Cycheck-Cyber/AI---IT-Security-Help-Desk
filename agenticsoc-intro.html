The Problem
I
AI Is Deployed Faster Than It Is Assured
Chatbots and voicebots are reaching customers before anyone has tested them against prompt injection, jailbreaks, data leakage, or misuse. Buyers are asking hard questions — and receiving reassuring narratives instead of evidence.
II
Generic Pentests Miss the AI Layer
Traditional application security coverage ends where the model begins. Adversarial prompts, context manipulation, social-engineering at scale, and agentic-action abuse all sit in a gap that standard testing does not reach.
III
Compliance Without Evidence Is a Liability
GDPR and the EU AI Act expect documented, reproducible evidence of AI risk control. A well-meaning policy document is not the same as a timestamped assessment with findings, remediations, and reviewer sign-off.
What AgenticSOC Is

An assurance service for the agentic era

AgenticSOC is a European assurance service for AI-powered customer-facing systems — chatbots, voicebots, and the emerging class of agentic systems that take actions on a user's behalf. We assess them the way a real attacker and a real regulator would, and we hand the result back as a report you can defend.

Each engagement is scoped in plain language, executed under a controlled assurance process, and delivered as structured findings with severity, evidence, and recommended remediation. Nothing leaves our platform without a human review. Nothing reaches the customer without explicit sign-off.

01
Scoped in Conversation
Engagements begin as a guided conversation, not a 40-page questionnaire. We capture the target, the intended use, the industry context, and the constraints in a single session — and confirm scope in writing before any assessment begins.
02
Executed Against Real Attack Patterns
Assessments draw on current AI-specific methodology — OWASP LLM Top 10, MITRE ATLAS, GDPR, the EU AI Act — and are tailored to how your bot actually behaves. We test what your system does, not what a generic checklist assumes.
03
Reviewed Before It Leaves Us
Every finding is reviewed inside AgenticSOC before it reaches you. Severity, framework mapping, and remediation guidance are validated against the actual context of your system — not produced in isolation by a single tool.
04
Delivered as Defensible Evidence
You receive a structured report with severity-ranked findings, framework references, remediation actions, and a timestamped audit record. Designed to be handed straight to your auditor, your board, and your regulator — not translated first.
